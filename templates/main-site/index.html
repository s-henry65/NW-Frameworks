Northwest Frameworks is a custom wholesale picture frame provider, serving framers
and artists in the Pacific Northwest and beyond.

All our work is of the highest quality, milled in our own shop from raw lumber in walnut, cherry, maple, and ash. Each frame features beautiful splined joinery and finishes ranging from natural wood and selected stains to the finest water-gilded 12K and 22K gold leaf. With two gold options and three colors of clay bole to choose from, we can offer our framing clients a wide palette of color and design options for gilded work.

Dane B. Wilson has been gilding, framing, and building high-calibre art displays on the west coast for over thirty years.

Please contact us for wholesale inquiries, pricing and sample sets. We can setup your account and provide login credentials.
